Kebab

This kebab is often served with traditional accompaniments like flatbread, saffron rice, grilled tomatoes, and fresh herbs. It's a popular dish at gatherings and celebrations, showcasing the rich culinary heritage of Iran. Enjoy the delightful taste of Iranian Koobideh Kebab with friends and family!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef or lamb
  • 1 large onion, grated
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon ground sumac
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Olive oil (for brushing)
