Pizza

This pizza is a celebration of fresh, seasonal ingredients. The earthy, savory notes of the mushrooms mingle with the sweet burst of cherry tomatoes, creating a flavor combination that's both comforting and exciting. The gooey mozzarella cheese brings it all together, making this pizza a surefire crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)
