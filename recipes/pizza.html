Pizza

This pizza is a celebration of fresh, seasonal ingredients. The earthy, savory notes of the mushrooms mingle with the sweet burst of cherry tomatoes, creating a flavor combination that's both comforting and exciting. The gooey mozzarella cheese brings it all together, making this pizza a surefire crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • Fresh basil leaves, for garnish
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Crushed red pepper flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven according to the instructions on the pizza dough package or to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Prepare the Dough: Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. If you're using store-bought dough, follow the package instructions.
  3. Assemble the Pizza: Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza stone or baking sheet. Brush the surface with olive oil to prevent sogginess. Spread an even layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  4. Add Cheese and Toppings: Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. Distribute the sliced mushrooms and halved cherry tomatoes on top. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Bake in the Oven: Transfer the pizza to the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Garnish: Once out of the oven, garnish your pizza with fresh basil leaves. If you like a bit of heat, sprinkle some crushed red pepper flakes over the top.
  7. Slice and Serve: Allow the pizza to cool for a few minutes before slicing. Serve your Mushroom and Cherry Tomato Pizza hot and enjoy!
